### Palace of Darkness

I'm going to give this a segment ✅ for the hover route, which a couple of players have proved can be used in runs and is in the game's top two runs.

Another fun note: when I started, almost every player did the "old" small key route:
- 1st trip to Junction
- Small key on the bridge in Bumper Beetles
- Return to Junction and bomb the cracked bridge
- Big Key
- Fall to turtle key for the small key there
- Mirror and go to hammer

The current route was found by acmlm in either early 2013 or 2012, so it was known when I started. But the only run I knew of that did it was Krystal's PB. In a fun bit of irony, even acmlm didn't do this route lol. It wasn't until godtas5 that Jadin thoroughly tested it and started doing acmlm's route himself that the meta fully changed over to acmlm's route. Due to all of this, I'm going to give it another segment ✅
